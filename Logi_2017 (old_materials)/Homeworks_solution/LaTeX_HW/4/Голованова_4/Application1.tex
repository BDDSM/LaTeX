\section{THE LIST OF NECESSARY BOOKS AND ITEMS}
  \vspace{1cm}
  
  UNIFORM 
  
\begin{description}[font=\normalfont\textitplusparen]
\item  Three sets of plain work robes (black)
\item  One plain pointed hat (black) for day wear
\item  One pair of protective gloves (dragon hide or similar)
\item  One winter cloak (black, with silver fastenings)
\end{description}
Please note that all pupil's clothes should carry name tags.
\vspace{1cm} 

COURSE BOOKS

\begin{description}[font=\normalfont\textitplusparen]
\item  The Standard Book of Spells (Grade 1)
by Miranda Goshawk

\item  A History of Magic
by Bathilda Bagshot

\item  Magical Theory
by Adalbert Waffling

\item  A Beginner's Guide to Transfiguration
by Emeric Switch

\item  One Thousand Magical Herbs and Fungi
by Phyllida Spore

\item Magical Drafts and Potions
by Arsenius Jigger

\item Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them
by Newt Scamander

\item The Dark Forces: A Guide to Self-Protection
by Quentin Trimble
\end{description}
\vspace{1cm} 

OTHER EQUIPMENT
\begin{description}[font=\normalfont\textitplusparen]
\item 1 wand
\item 1 cauldron (pewter, standard size 2)
\item 1 set glass or crystal phials
\item 1 telescope
\item 1 set brass scales
\end{description}

Students may also bring, if they desire, an owl OR a cat OR a toad.
